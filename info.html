club information

astronomy club was started by Dillon Gordon and Chloe Orrick--if there are any questions feel free to email us at orrickc1011@student.sanjuan.edu. we will try to answer questions as quickly as possible!

about the club: during the course of the club, we will be covering topics such as the mechanics behind stars, planets, black holes, galaxies & more, as well as having open discussions about information you will have learned after attending each meeting. we will also be watching videos about space to supplement information with cool visuals!!
